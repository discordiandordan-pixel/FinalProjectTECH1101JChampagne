Tool Centre Point (TCP) Demonstration

A live demonstration of defining and verifying a Tool Centre Point on an ABB industrial robot ("Bender") in the SAIT Mechatronics Laboratory. TCP calibration is a fundamental step before any precision robotic task, and must be redone whenever a new end-effector or tool is mounted to the robot's wrist.
